About Us

Upstricke is a video analysis solution that allows analysts to capture, analyze and share videos of training sessions and matches. The software offers tools to capture the footage directly into the platform, tag events real-time, and upload, organize and share the various videos produced , Its Software is used by athletic coaches to break down and analyze the movements as well as categorize videos to create an index of events (e.g. pass goal, player, etc.). The software uses digital video graphics to deliver instant visual.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the world with the best free video instruction for every sport. By best we mean best coaches, best production and best user experience “easy to find, search and navigate website”.

Vission

Our vision is provides a rich set of opportunities for the application of computer vision techniques to help the competitors, coaches and audience. This program discusses a selection of current commercial applications that use computer vision for sports analysis, and highlights some of the topics that are currently being addressed in the research community.

Passion

We believe in opportunity and data accessibility for all levels. Hence we provide a holistic approach to athlete and coach engagement. Our innovative sports analytic software aids you in improving performance and producing enhanced results.
